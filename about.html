BIOGRAPHY

Jonathan Gomez is a graphic design student at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas, currently in their fourth year of pursuing his Bachelors in Fine Arts. Passionate about visual storytelling and creative problem-solving, they have developed a strong foundation in digital illustration, branding, typography, and user experience (UX) design.

Throughout their academic journey he has gained hands-on experience through studio courses, collaborative projects, and internships that bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world application. Their design work reflects a thoughtful balance of creativity and functionality, with an emphasis on clean aesthetics, user engagement, and purposeful communication.
